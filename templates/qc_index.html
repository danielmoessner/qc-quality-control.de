In Liquidation

Dear customer,

I would like to thank you today for the many years of good cooperation regarding all QC products and to inform you that I will soon be retiring.
My company QC-Quality Control GmbH i.L. will go into liquidation on January 1, 2025 and thus cease its business activities. All open orders will of course be processed and delivered as before - the liquidation is expected to end on December 31, 2025

To all those interested in RION particle counters and SIMCO/ION ionization devices:

In order to ensure a continuous supply of the RION particle counter and SIMCO/ION ionization devices that you value, the company PMT Partikel-Messtechnik GmbH will now take over all customers and provide support in the future (from January 1, 2025). PMT Partikel-Messtechnik GmbH will contact you shortly and introduce you to their company.

  • Please visit their website at https://www.pmt.eu

If you have any further questions, please contact:
Dirk Jessen, CEO

  • +49 (0) 7033/5374-16
  • d.jessen@pmt.eu
